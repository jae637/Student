EI-SC401 high sensitivity handheld metal detector able to detected the metal with the principle of electromagnetic induction, simply operation, high sensitive, a tiny metal piece like a paper clip can be detected, with sound, buzz and light alarm functions.

Auto fault diagnosis without adjustable
Sound, buzz and light alarm models
High, middle and low sensitivity adjustable. Super sensitive like a tiny metal piece paper clip can be detected
The shell use engineering plastics, powerful ability of waterproof, fireproof, anticorrosive and shockproof
