EI-MD3000A

 

Product Instruction

EI-MD3000A high sensitivity digital walkthrough metal detector use excellent electromagnetic testing and digital processing technology, has high detection sensitivity of the magnetic metal and nonmagnetic metal, and good recognition for personal items such as coins, keys, belt buckle, etc.
multi-layered overlapping coil design, with high-brightness LEDs to show the horizontal position of metal objects accurately, which can be easy for operator to make judgment.
